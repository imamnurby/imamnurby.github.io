- Coordinator of Embedded System Laboratory Works, School of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, Jan 2019 - May 2019
- Laboratory Assistant of Digital System Design, School of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, Aug 2018 - Des 2018
- IoT Engineer Intern, PT Lentera Bumi Nusantara, May 2018 - Aug 2018

My research interest lies between the intersection of data science or artificial intelligence and software engineering, especially in its application in the robotics field. The emerging of robotic applications in various aspects is undeniable, and this trend will keep increasing in the future. Please look forward for my first publication!
